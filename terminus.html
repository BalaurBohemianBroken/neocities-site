Rover - Game about controlling a rover from the inside, programming every function. Inspired by Duskers, Outer Wilds, and the Opportunity rover. Stopped when I learned about Iron Lung.

are we still friends - I can't link this one because it doxxes me. Similar to That Binds but much earlier. Game about suicide, you would explore the room, and especially computer, of someone who has gone missing. Inspired by Return Of The Obra Dinn.

False Dream - This is my "big one". My default project since 2019. It's gone thorugh four major stages that could each be considered its own game. I'm not linking it because I still work on it. If you've heard me discuss volcanos, ice ages or hyper light drifter, it was this game.

Beautiful Water - Just something that got popular. I liked building river-side dwellings in RimWorld, and I wanted my characters to enjoy the view.

Rimworld replay - The commissioner ghosted me on this one, but I was passionate about it. Would record inputs and world state so you could rewatch your runs. Didn't finish.

Physical Research - Failed mod. Idea was to make research a resource that could be gained and lost, physically tied to people. Makes old people have more value.

I'm Off - An event to get rid of a colonist and keep everyone happy. I hated turning people down, but some really didn't fit in.

Math! - First large coding project I did. I would write pseudocode on my phone during the 16 hour bus up to Scotland. Learning to Harmony patch was a thrill.

PZ Character randomizer - Improved logic and settings for character randomization. I try not to min-max my characters.

PZ Maps - More paper-based maps for the game. I don't like auto-maps, I love annotating maps.

PZ Better Crafting - I didn't release this one. It was an ambitious mod that reworked the crafting system from the ground up. Because it was ass. To memory I nigh finished v1, but there was an update that would break everything. They also ignored my job application.

OwO Stawdew Vawwey - Silly mod, but very heartfelt. The codebase is stuffed with poetry and thoughts and feelings. Also made a dumb video for its release.

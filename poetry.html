I started poetry because I needed to say something really complicated and far reaching to someone, and they didn't read it. So I wanted to get better.

I go in to poetry needing to express something. I put obsessions in a document, I stir them around with songs and books and games I like until none of my friends will know what I'm talking about anymore. Once I've expressed what I needed to, the poem is 'done' and I stop in whatever state it's in. Maybe that's charm, maybe that's unprofessional.

Most of my poems embarrass me. Some are bad. Most aren't here. I getting better.

I've read almost no poetry. That may be obvious. I draw a lot from music. That may not be obvious.

Writing got a lot freer when I realized half of my friends read half of my poetry, and half of them think twice about it. Suddenly, I was liberated to start referencing them and things they knew, without worrying I'd hurt anyone.

I get that poetry isn't for most people. In fact, my poems aren't for you either, they're for me.
But if you get something out if it... thanks.

Now I just gotta figure out how I want to put them on here.
